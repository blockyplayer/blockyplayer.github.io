Epic Combat

Epic Combat is an action-RPG game where you can prove yourself as a mighty adventurer; raid epicly-large dungeones and labyrinths, fight competitors and enemies to the death, and build forts and castles to protect your allies whilst ridding the world of scum, no-good mobs. Fight the Hungry, a dangerous plant capable of more than meets the eye; the Dungeon Master, who can kill you in multiple different worlds with dangerous fireballs; and the Frostbiter, an icy foe that is very hard to defeat! Kill the Skull King and take it's crown to prove yourself superior to others! We hope you enjoy this epic selection of mods to aid you in your adventure :-D
